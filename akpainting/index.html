About Us

Akash Painting & Decorating LTD is a trusted residential and commercial painting company providing professional interior and exterior painting services. We specialize in wall painting, lacquer and stain finishes, kitchen cabinet refinishing, stucco painting, drywall patching, and oak-to-white trim transformations. From preparation to final detailing, we deliver clean, high-quality workmanship.
